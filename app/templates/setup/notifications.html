How Notifications Are Delivered

🔔
Notification Bell Active
Every notification appears in the bell icon (🔔) in the top navigation bar. Employees, managers, and admins each see only the notifications relevant to them. Unread count is shown as a badge. Clicking the bell opens a panel listing all recent notifications with a link to the relevant ticket or checklist.
⚡
Real-Time Updates Active
Notifications are pushed instantly to the browser without needing a page refresh. If an employee has OmniFlow open on their screen, the bell badge updates and a toast appears the moment a ticket is assigned, a checklist goes overdue, or a TaT threshold is crossed — no manual refresh required.
