Available KPIs — What each metric measures
{# Delegation #}
Delegation (Tickets)
📅 Ticket On-Time Rate
What % of assigned tickets were completed before their due date. Why it matters: directly reflects deadline discipline — the most visible signal of team reliability.
✅ Ticket Completion Rate
What % of created tickets eventually get closed. Why it matters: catches stale or abandoned work — a team that starts but never finishes harms throughput.
{# Checklist #}
Checklists
📋 Checklist Compliance
What % of scheduled checklists were completed (vs skipped/missed). Why it matters: recurring procedures exist for safety and quality — skipping them is the highest-risk behaviour to track.
⏱ Checklist On-Time Rate
Of the checklists that were completed, what % were done before their deadline. Why it matters: separates teams that complete routines promptly from those who complete them late.
{# FMS #}
Flow Management System (FMS)
🔄 FMS Stage On-Time Rate
What % of FMS tickets had all of the employee's assigned stages completed within each stage's individual TaT window — measured from when the employee actually received the work. Why it matters: holds each person accountable only for the stages they worked on, not delays caused by other team members in the same ticket.
