Security Notes

  • Super admin accounts use a separate JWT secret and cookie from factory users — there is no cross-access.
  • The SA portal is accessible at /superadmin/login — consider restricting this path to known IPs in production.
  • Deactivated accounts are immediately locked — their active sessions expire within 8 hours (token TTL).
  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt and never stored in plaintext.
  • To recover a forgotten password, another active SA can deactivate + recreate the account, or a developer can run reset_sa_password.py from the server.
